WELCOME TO FANMADE WIKIA OF WINGS OF GLORY

This is a fanmade wikia page for the roblox game, Wings of Glory. The game is an arcade air combat simulation with over 130 aircrafts from early WWII planes to nowadays modern jets.

Nations

There are total 6 nations in the game currently. Each of them has uniquie aircrafts and armaments contribute to each nation's military doctrine. There is also separated Special and Event tree for aircrafts from other nations.
